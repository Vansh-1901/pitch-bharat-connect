
# Karo Pitch Landing Page

## Overview
A modern, clean startup-style landing page for "Karo Pitch" — a platform by KaroStartup connecting early-stage Indian founders with investors.

## Design Direction
- Minimal, modern UI inspired by Product Hunt / Y Combinator aesthetics
- Color palette: Deep indigo/violet primary with warm orange accents, soft gradient backgrounds
- Professional typography with clean spacing
- Rounded cards, subtle shadows, smooth scroll animations
- Fully mobile responsive

## Sections (single-page layout)

### 1. Hero Section
- Bold headline: "Pitch Your Startup to India's Top Investors"
- Supporting subtext about the platform
- Two CTA buttons: "Apply to Pitch" (primary) and "Explore Startups" (outline)
- Abstract gradient background with floating geometric shapes

### 2. About Karo Pitch
- Brief explanation of the access gap for Tier-2/3 founders
- Clean two-column layout with text and a visual element

### 3. How It Works
- 4-step horizontal process with icons (ClipboardList, CheckCircle, Presentation, Rocket)
- Connected with a subtle progress line
- Each step has icon, title, and short description

### 4. Who Can Apply
- 6 category cards in a responsive grid (D2C Brands, Consumer Startups, MSMEs, SaaS, Manufacturing, Bharat-focused)
- Each with an icon and label

### 5. Investors Section
- Headline about meeting investors
- Placeholder investor profile cards with avatars and roles

### 6. Featured Startups
- 4 startup cards (FarmKart, GlowLeaf, BuildEase, QuickKart)
- Each showing name, category badge, and short description

### 7. About KaroStartup
- Brief section about the parent platform's mission and community

### 8. Final CTA
- Bold headline "Ready to Pitch Your Startup?"
- Two buttons: "Apply Now" and "Partner With Us"
- Gradient background to stand out

### 9. Footer
- Simple footer with links and copyright
